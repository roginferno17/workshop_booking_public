Why FOSSEE?

Software Education, Reimagined

FOSSEE promotes the use of free & open source software in academic institutions across India.

Zero Cost

All workshops are completely free. No registration fees, no hidden charges — ever. Funded by the MHRD, Govt. of India.

Expert Instructors

Learn directly from IIT Bombay faculty and trained FOSSEE team members who build and maintain these tools daily.

Hands-On Learning

Not just lectures — each workshop includes live coding exercises, real datasets, and practical problem-solving sessions.
